The contemporary reinterpretation of vernacular traditions and techniques.

The most inspiring movement in architecture is the revival of vernacular practices, and their reintegration into the mainstream of contemporary architecture.

New Vernacular will showcase this synthesis by highlighting projects that successfully reinterpret vernacular forms, materials, and techniques.

What We Do

Connect

We connect African, Latin American, and Southeast Asian culture with the western world.

Discover

We showcase the works of architects, designers, and artists who typically don’t get exposure in popular culture or mainstream platforms.

Rewrite

We believe that our platform will become a tool to rewrite the narrative of mainstream design.
